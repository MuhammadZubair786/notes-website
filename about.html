INTRODUCTION

Notes World is an online academic website which is launched to facilitate the students through our various online educational services. The main and foremost objective of this website is to provide free, latest, updated, easy, comprehensive and lucid Notes for the young (future) generation, so that they can get benefit by studying these notes. We can assure that by studying these notes, students can get maximum marks in their exams which will brighten their future.

Keeping in view that present era is technological period particularly in the field of information technology, we are also providing the Notes/Handouts, Tips and Tricks, Video Tutorials (CBTs) to our beloved students, so that they can make progress in the field of Information Technology as without proper IT Education, we could not progress in any walk of life.

Furthermore, We also offer the services to find the Home Tutors, Tuition Centers and Academies on the click of the button. This feature also provide the facility to the teachers to register themselves free of cost without paying any Commission and post their Ad, so that students can find them.
